## 👤 About Me

I am an **AI Engineer and PhD candidate** at Ankara University (Computer Engineering), specializing in **Generative AI, Large Language Models (LLMs), and agentic AI systems** — with a dedicated research focus on **AI security, adversarial robustness, and trustworthy AI deployment**.

I design and build production-grade GenAI pipelines — RAG systems, LLMOps workflows, fine-tuned models, and agentic architectures — while simultaneously researching how these systems can be made more secure and resilient against emerging threats.

A background in cybersecurity and NATO-aligned advisory roles gives me an adversarial mindset that informs how I engineer AI: I build with attack surfaces in mind.

> *"Designing and securing the architecture of next-generation GenAI and agentic systems — driven by deep academic curiosity and research into trustworthy AI."*
